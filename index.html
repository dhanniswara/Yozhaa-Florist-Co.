Our Story

More than flowers —
an emotion, composed

Founded in the lush archipelago of Indonesia, Yozhaa began as an obsession: to bring the precision of haute couture to the ephemeral art of floristry. We source blooms from the finest growers across Java, Bali, and beyond — selecting only those that meet our exacting standard of beauty.

Each arrangement is conceived as a composition. We consider proportion, texture, fragrance, and the slow theatre of blooming. Our studio is a place where nature is curated, never forced.

— Yozhaa, Indonesia
